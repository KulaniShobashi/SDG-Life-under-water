Critical Marine Habitats

Coral Reefs

Support 25% of marine species despite covering less than 1% of ocean area. Threatened by warming waters and acidification.

Kelp Forests

Underwater forests providing shelter and food for diverse marine life. Declining due to warming waters and sea urchin outbreaks.

Seagrass Meadows

Nursery grounds for fish and carbon storage systems. Threatened by coastal development and water pollution.

Deep Sea

Earth's largest habitat, largely unexplored. Facing threats from deep-sea mining and climate change.
