ABOUT US

Our Mission

UMI EDU advances UN Sustainable Development Goal 14 through evidence-based marine conservation. We address the urgent challenges facing our oceans: 37.7% of fish stocks are overfished, ocean acidity has increased 30% since pre-industrial times, and marine ecosystems face unprecedented threats. Through research, education, and community engagement, we work to achieve the SDG 14 targets for sustainable ocean use and conservation.

Advancing SDG 14 Targets

Marine Pollution Research

Researching marine debris, plastic pollution, and coastal eutrophication to support SDG 14.1 target of preventing and reducing marine pollution by 2025.

Ocean Acidification Monitoring

Contributing to the global network of 765 monitoring stations tracking ocean pH decline and supporting SDG 14.3 efforts to minimize acidification impacts.

Sustainable Fisheries

Supporting small-scale fisheries and combating illegal fishing to achieve SDG 14.4 and 14.b targets for sustainable fish stock management and fisher access rights.
