Our Mission

UMI EDU is dedicated to raising awareness about marine conservation and the critical importance of protecting our oceans. Through education and advocacy, we work to achieve UN Sustainable Development Goal 14 and ensure healthy marine ecosystems for future generations.

Our Vision

A world where oceans thrive with abundant marine life, where sustainable practices protect underwater ecosystems, and where every person understands their role in ocean conservation.
